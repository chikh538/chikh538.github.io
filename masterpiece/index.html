TIPS FOR ARTISTS
  WHO WANT TO SELL






  John Baldessari, 1966-68











  



  
1

  
  
    Generally speaking, paintings with
    light colors sell more quickly than
    paintings with dark
  
  







  






  
2

  
  
    Subjects that Sell Well:
    Madonna and Child, Landscapes, Flower Paintings,
    Still Lifes (Free of Morbid Props - - - Dead Birds, etc.).
    Nudes, Marine Picture, Abstracts and Surrealism.
  
  









  
3

  
  
    Subject Matter is Important:
    It has been said that paintings with cows and
    hens in them collect dust - - - while the same
    paintings with bulls and roosters sell.
